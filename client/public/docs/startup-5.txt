EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

CleanSea develops autonomous underwater drones (AUVs) equipped with AI for ocean
cleanup and marine biodiversity restoration. Our technology removes plastic waste,
monitors coral health, and tracks endangered species at scale.

Vision: Restore ocean health through intelligent automation
Mission: Deploy 1,000 autonomous cleaners in oceans by 2030

FUNDING GOAL: 25 ETH (~$75,000 USD)
FUNDS ALLOCATION:
  - Hardware R&D: 40% (10 ETH)
  - Manufacturing Setup: 25% (6.25 ETH)

================================================================================
PRODUCT & TECHNOLOGY
================================================================================

Core Products:

1. CleanBot AUV (Autonomous Underwater Vehicle)
   - Length: 1.5 meters, weight: 45 kg
   - Battery: 72-hour endurance
   - Collection mechanism: Net with AI-powered object recognition
   - Payload capacity: 50 kg of plastic waste
   - Operating depth: 0-300 meters
   - Speed: 2-6 knots

2. Ocean Monitoring System
   - Real-time water quality sensors (pH, temperature, salinity, turbidity)
   - Marine species identification via computer vision
   - Coral bleaching detection algorithm (95% accuracy)
   - Microplastic concentration mapping

3. Command & Control Platform
   - Fleet management dashboard
   - Mission planning and optimization
   - Real-time telemetry and video feeds
   - Automated obstacle avoidance
   - Data analytics and reporting

Technology Stack:
  - Autonomous Navigation: ROS2 + SLAM algorithms
  - AI: TensorFlow for marine object detection
  - Hardware: NVIDIA Jetson for edge computing
  - Communication: Acoustic modem + satellite relay
  - Energy: LiPo batteries + solar charging stations
