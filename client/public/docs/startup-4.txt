EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

HealthLoop is a telemedicine platform connecting rural patients in East Africa
with qualified specialist doctors. We bridge the 85% doctor shortage in rural
areas through video consultations, digital prescriptions, and follow-up care.

Vision: Every person deserves access to quality healthcare
Mission: Connect rural communities with specialist care

FUNDING GOAL: 10 ETH (~$30,000 USD)
FUNDS ALLOCATION:
  - Technology Infrastructure: 35% (3.5 ETH)
  - Doctor Network Expansion: 30% (3 ETH)
  - Patient Acquisition: 20% (2 ETH)
  - Regulatory/Operations: 15% (1.5 ETH)

================================================================================
MARKET OPPORTUNITY
================================================================================

Total Addressable Market (TAM): $8.2B (East African healthcare)
Target Market: 65M rural residents in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda
Current Penetration: <2% use telemedicine services

Market Drivers:
  - 85% of specialist doctors concentrated in urban areas
  - Rural healthcare infrastructure critically underfunded
  - Smartphone penetration rising (60%+ in target areas)
  - Mobile money adoption enables healthcare payments
  - Government commitment to universal health coverage

Competitive Advantages:
  ✓ Offline prescription delivery (SMS-based in low-connectivity areas)
  ✓ Lowest cost consultations ($2-5 vs. $25+ traditional clinics)
  ✓ Integration with local health workers and CHVs
  ✓ Support for multiple African languages

================================================================================
PRODUCT & TECHNOLOGY
================================================================================

Core Services:

1. Video Consultations
   - Real-time specialist consultations
   - Low-bandwidth optimization for rural areas
   - Recording capability for follow-ups
   - Average wait time: 8 minutes

2. Digital Prescriptions & Fulfillment
   - Digital prescription delivery
   - Integration with 1,200+ pharmacies
   - Prescription tracking and follow-up
   - SMS reminders for medication adherence

3. Chronic Disease Management
   - Ongoing monitoring for diabetes, hypertension
   - Automated refills and reminders
   - Patient education content
   - Integration with home-based test devices

4. Health Records
   - Secure patient health records
   - Interoperability with clinics
   - Portable medical history

Tech Stack:
  - Mobile: React Native (iOS/Android)
  - Backend: Node.js, PostgreSQL
  - Infrastructure: AWS + local cloud (low latency)
  - Offline-first architecture for connectivity issues
  - Security: HIPAA-equivalent standards

================================================================================
RISK & MITIGATION
================================================================================

Regulatory Risk:
  Risk: Medical licensing requirements across countries
  Mitigation: All doctors licensed; compliant with each country's standards

Infrastructure Risk:
  Risk: Connectivity limitations in rural areas
  Mitigation: Offline-capable app; SMS fallback systems

Payment Risk:
  Risk: Low payment capacity in rural areas
  Mitigation: Subsidized pricing; partnerships with health programs
