EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

NeuroFlow AI is a cognitive wellness platform powered by advanced AI algorithms
that provides real-time mental health insights and personalized interventions.
Our technology combines neuroscience with machine learning to detect early signs
of mental health challenges and deliver proactive support.

Vision: To democratize access to mental health care through intelligent technology
Mission: Empower individuals with personalized cognitive wellness tools

PRODUCT & TECHNOLOGY
================================================================================

Core Features:
1. AI-Powered Daily Assessment
   - Natural language processing for mood analysis
   - Wearable data integration for biometric insights
   - Real-time anomaly detection

2. Personalized Interventions
   - CBT-based exercises adapted to user patterns
   - Peer support group recommendations
   - Integration with licensed therapists

3. Progress Tracking Dashboard
   - 30-day, 90-day, annual wellness trends
   - Predictive analytics for mental health trajectory
   - Shareable reports for healthcare providers

Technical Stack:
  - Backend: Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL
  - ML: TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn
  - Frontend: React, TypeScript, Mobile (React Native)
  - Infrastructure: AWS, Docker, Kubernetes
  - Security: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001
